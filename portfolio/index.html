Introduction

Hi, Abhinith here,

I have always wanted to work in a real environment and experience it for myself, and this enthusiasm led me to study for a bachelor's degree in Statistics and a masters in Big Data Analytics.I have always followed the technological advancements around me, whether it's any new smartphone or laptop in the market, I always dive into the specifications to understand the key differences. The desire to understand how things work and the meaning of specific technologies has always made me challenge myself, and continue to learn and develop in my personal and professional life.

Getting to help people with the data they provided, give them the best insights, helping them solve their business problem, were all the things that still motivates me to pursue a career in data science. I’ve always enjoyed solving puzzles, finding patters taking up new challenges and conquer them just gives me the best satisfaction ever

Hardest decision that I had to take was on choosing what to take for masters. I had a lot of suggestions and lot of paths open. Rather than going to a business school or taking pure statistics .My thoughts drifted on doing a specialization in analytics .So here I am doing my Masters in Big Data Analytics, doing what I love and perusing my passion. It was a topsy–turvy ride till then, now I do feel happy on taking this decision. Hoping to help people and organizations with the skill that I have acquired over the past few years .
